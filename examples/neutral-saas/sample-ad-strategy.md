# OpenGrowth Ad Strategy Blueprint (Curated Sample)

> [!NOTE]
> This is a curated sample report. The full OpenGrowth tool generates a larger set of hook variations, video concepts, and copy templates.

## 1. Audience Segments

### Segment A: Overwhelmed Startup Founders
* **Stage:** Top of Funnel (TOFU)
* **Pain Point:** Spending hours parsing customer requests manually in spreadsheets.
* **Focus:** Save time and centralize prioritization.

### Segment B: Product Managers at Agencies
* **Stage:** Middle of Funnel (MOFU)
* **Pain Point:** Client and stakeholder roadmap debates are subjective and loud.
* **Focus:** Objective prioritizing data models (RICE scores).

### Segment C: Customer Success Leads
* **Stage:** Bottom of Funnel (BOFU)
* **Pain Point:** Customers churn because they feel their requests are ignored.
* **Focus:** Automated feedback notifications and loops.

## 2. Ad Hook Variations

* **Hook 1 (Pain-Based)**: "Still copying customer requests from Slack into a messy spreadsheet?"
* **Hook 2 (Gain-Based)**: "Turn customer feedback into structured, prioritized roadmap decisions."
* **Hook 3 (Feature-Based)**: "Slack integration + RICE scoring = organized product roadmap in minutes."
* **Hook 4 (Social Proof)**: "The tool product teams use to stop roadmap debates."
* **Hook 5 (Urgency-Based)**: "Stop guessing what feature to build next. Get started for free."
