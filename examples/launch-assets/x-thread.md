# Twitter/X Launch Thread Draft

A 7-10 post thread introducing OpenGrowth, structured to be engaging and informative.

---

### **Tweet 1 (Intro)**
We spend months building products, only to launch and wonder why visitors aren't converting. 

Meet OpenGrowth: an open-source, local-first growth operating system that audits your website for SEO, trust, conversion leaks, and ad/content strategies. 🧵 👇

---

### **Tweet 2 (The Lighthouse analogy)**
Think of OpenGrowth as Lighthouse, but for growth. 

Instead of measuring page load speed, it parses your landing page copy, headers, and metadata, evaluating it against a rule-based engine to score your website's marketing readiness.

---

### **Tweet 3 (Local & Free)**
OpenGrowth is 100% local-first and privacy-friendly. 

No paid API keys, no monthly SaaS subscriptions, and no sending your proprietary website data to external servers. It runs entirely on your own machine.

---

### **Tweet 4 (Detailed Reports)**
Every audit generates a suite of 9 output files:
- Interactive single-file `report.html` 📊
- Actionable next-step `report.md` 📝
- Detailed `scorecard.json` metrics 📉
- Raw parsed copy and rules databases.

---

### **Tweet 5 (Marketing Planner)**
OpenGrowth goes beyond audits to suggest strategies:
- Groups page keywords into topic clusters.
- Generates a 30-day multi-channel content calendar.
- Recommends distribution ideas based on your website's content gaps.

---

### **Tweet 6 (Ad Angle Generator)**
Need ad copy? 

OpenGrowth extracts your product value propositions and generates platform-specific ad hooks, pain/gain variants, reel concepts, and carousel outlines. Ready to copy-paste.

---

### **Tweet 7 (GitHub Action & Dashboard)**
Automate it.
- Spin up the local self-hosted dashboard to run audits visually and view history.
- Add our composite GitHub Action to your CI pipeline to fail PRs if a code change drops your growth score.

---

### **Tweet 8 (v0.9 Release)**
With the v0.9 examples release, we've included FlowPilot: a fictional B2B SaaS demo context and curated audit report files. 

You can inspect the sample reports and test commands without setting up a full workspace.

---

### **Tweet 9 (Ethical Open Source)**
OpenGrowth is built on transparency:
- Free & open-source (MIT).
- Clear, rule-based scoring (no AI black box).
- Factual growth analysis.

Check out the repository, star it to follow development, and contribute rules! 👇
