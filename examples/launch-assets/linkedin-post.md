## Post Content

We spend weeks building product features, writing clean code, and preparing deployment pipelines. 

But when it comes to launching, we often skip the basics of growth engineering. 

Did we register a clear value proposition? Are our headings structured for search engines? Do we have essential trust signals like privacy policies, SSL, and contact paths? 

To solve this, I built **OpenGrowth** — a developer-first, open-source Growth Operating System.

Think of it as Lighthouse, but instead of scoring page speed, it audits search optimization, conversion paths, trust factors, and copy clarity. 

Beyond auditing, it creates actionable assets directly from your crawled content:
- 📅 A 30-day content strategy calendar.
- 🎯 Ready-to-use ad copy hooks (pain, gain, feature, and social proof angles).
- 📊 Standalone visual HTML and Markdown reports.
- 🐳 A self-hosted dashboard to run audits visually and track historical reports.

The best part? It's entirely free, runs locally on your machine, and requires no paid AI or API keys. You can run it as a CLI tool or plug it into your GitHub Actions to block PRs that lower your conversion score.

OpenGrowth v0.9 is live today with new examples and launch assets. Check out the project, and let me know how you audit your websites before launching!
