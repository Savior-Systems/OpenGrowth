# OpenGrowth Product Strategy

> Internal planning document — public-facing but strategy-focused.

## Positioning

**OpenGrowth** is the open-source Growth Operating System that audits any website and turns it into a content strategy, ad strategy, conversion diagnosis, and execution roadmap.

### One-Line Pitch
> Lighthouse for growth, content, ads, SEO/AEO, and conversion.

### Target Users

| Segment | Need | OpenGrowth Value |
|---------|------|-----------------|
| Solo Developers | Ship growth-ready products | Automated audit before launch |
| Indie Founders | Growth without a marketing team | Actionable strategy from a URL |
| Marketers | Quick competitor/client audits | Instant structured reports |
| Agencies | Scalable client audits | CLI + CI/CD pipeline integration |
| Open-Source Maintainers | Better project pages | README and landing page scoring |

## Competitive Landscape

| Tool | Type | Cost | Depth |
|------|------|------|-------|
| Google Lighthouse | Open-source CLI | Free | Performance/SEO only |
| Ahrefs/SEMrush | SaaS | $99+/mo | SEO + limited content |
| Surfer SEO | SaaS | $89+/mo | Content only |
| OpenGrowth | Open-source CLI | Free | Growth + Content + Ads + Conversion |

### Differentiation
- **Free** vs. paid SaaS
- **Holistic** vs. single-dimension (SEO only, content only)
- **Local-first** vs. cloud-dependent
- **Developer-friendly** vs. marketer-only UIs
- **Extensible** via custom rule packs vs. closed systems

## Revenue Model (Future, Not v1)

OpenGrowth is MIT-licensed and free forever. Future monetization options (not planned for v1):
- Hosted dashboard (SaaS tier)
- Enterprise rule packs
- Managed audit-as-a-service
- Consulting and custom integrations

## Ethical Growth Principles

1. No fake stars or manufactured engagement
2. No spam in communities
3. Genuine value-first content
4. Transparent roadmap and decision-making
5. Credit contributors properly
6. Honest benchmarks and comparisons
